Tile Patterns

I'm getting my bathroom tiled and saw these cool semi-teardrop shaped tiles that I wanted to use in the shower, so I made this to help me visualise patterns for them.

The black line marks the corner between the two walls, and the black box is roughly where the shower unit will be. The height of the tiles is actually a fair bit taller than this, but I'm going to just have white on the bottom, since I only really care about a pattern for the bit that'll be in my eyeline.
