Explanation

Why?

Every year (as of 2018) during Lent I abstain from caffeine because I drink too much coffee and my heart can only tolerate so much.

This little app lets me know if I can drink coffee, and when I can drink it next if I can't.

Lent

Different religious denominations define Lent differently, but this is the ruleset I use:

Lent starts on Ash Wednesday (always the day after Pancake Day/Shrove Tuesday)

Lent ends 40 days after that.

However, as my birthday is on the 19th April, and Lent this year (2019) ends a couple days before my birthday, I'm contriving a rule to bump it up a few days longer so I can enjoy my first post-Lent coffee on my 26th birthday.
